Our Commitment to your Health and Safety

IF YOU ANSWERED YES TO ANY OF THE FOLLOWING QUESTIONS BELOW, DO NOT BOOK A WORKOUT OR ENTER THE PREMISES. SELF-ISOLATE IMMEDIATELY AND CONTACT YOUR DESIGNATED PUBLIC HEALTH AUTHORITY FOR FURTHER DIRECTION.

  1. Are you experiencing any of the following symptoms?
    • Fever above 38 C
    • New or worsening cough
    • Sore throat
    • Runny nose
    • Headache
    • Shortness of breath
    • New onset fatigue
    • New onset muscle pain
    • Diarrhea
    • Loss of taste or smell
    • Conjunctivitis
  2. Are you under the age of 18 and experiencing purple fingers or toes?
  3. Have you returned from unauthorized interprovincial travel in the last 14 days?
  4. Have you had close contact within the last 14 days with a confirmed case of COVID-19?
  5. Have you had close contact within the last 14 days with a person being tested for COVID-19?
